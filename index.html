Introduction

H&C Inc is a new call center that recently opened in Austin, Texas. I was hired as a Tableau Consultant to analyze their employee KPIs. The KPIs allow stakeholders to monitor progress, identify trends, and create an expansion plan for 2024.

Business Questions

  1. How does call volume vary during the week?
  2. What are the patterns in inbound calls and call duration?
  3. Which employees resolve the most calls?
  4. What is the overall resolution rate across employees?
  5. How efficient is the team’s speed of answer?

About the Data

Eight employees were randomly selected from various departments, and their performance was analyzed for call resolution, customer satisfaction, and speed of answer. About 5,000 calls were analyzed on these parameters.

Tools Used

  • Excel – Data Cleaning
  • Tableau – Visualization

Insights

  1. Daily inbound calls showed steady activity throughout the week, with slight peaks midweek and the highest on Mondays. The average call duration provided insight into employee efficiency.
  2. Two employees consistently achieved a higher number of resolved calls compared to their peers. A bar chart featuring the number of calls was very useful to narrow down high achievers.
  3. The resolution rate exceeded the team’s average benchmark, showing effective problem-solving across most employees.
  4. The team maintained a speed of answer above 60%, meaning more than half of incoming calls were answered promptly within the target timeframe.
  5. Customer satisfaction ratings averaged 4+ on a 5-point scale, showing a strong positive response to call handling quality.

Recommendations

  • Staffing: Allocate more agents to Mondays and high-volume periods. Employee start times should be staggered during the day to provide maximum coverage. Offer in-person, hybrid, and remote options to retain employees.
  • Training: Share best practices from top performers to boost team-wide resolution rates.
  • Monitoring: Track daily satisfaction and speed of answer metrics to maintain responsiveness.
  • Process Optimization: Continue analyzing call duration to balance speed and quality.
